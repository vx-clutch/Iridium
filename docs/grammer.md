# Grammar

## Hierarchy for Iridium
$$
\text{Program} \to \text{List of statements} \to \text{block} \to \left\{ 
\begin{matrix}
\text{operations} \\
\text{literals} \\
\text{variables}
\end{matrix} 
\right\}
$$

\end{matrix} 
\right\}
    \end{matrix}
    \right\}
$$
